We provide a structured accelerated transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) program in a supportive outpatient setting. Our team focuses on safety, comfort, and clear communication at every step.

Who accelerated TMS may be for

Adults with depression

For people with major depressive symptoms who have not had enough benefit from medications, therapy, or standard treatment timelines.

Patients needing faster structure

For those with time constraints who may prefer a concentrated schedule over a longer, spread-out course of care.

Out-of-area referrals

For patients traveling from other cities or states who need coordinated planning with their home providers.

Potential benefits

Why patients choose TMS

  • No anesthesia or sedation required
  • No systemic medication side effects
  • Sessions are brief and outpatient
  • Can be paired with ongoing psychotherapy

Our clinic approach

We combine psychiatric evaluation, protocol matching, daily symptom tracking, and regular clinical check-ins so care stays individualized throughout the treatment window.
