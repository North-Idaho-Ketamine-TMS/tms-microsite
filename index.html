Who accelerated TMS may fit

Treatment-resistant depression

Adults who have not reached their goals with medications, psychotherapy, or standard TMS pacing may discuss whether a condensed schedule is appropriate after full medical review.

Limited time on the calendar

Working professionals and travelers who prefer to complete a course in days rather than many weeks of daily commuting—when medically appropriate.

Out-of-area guests

Patients flying or driving to Coeur d’Alene for a structured intensive, with lodging near downtown and lake access between sessions. See our Travel Patients page.
