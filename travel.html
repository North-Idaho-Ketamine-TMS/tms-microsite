Out-of-area care

Travel and lodging guidance for visiting patients

We support patients and families coming from outside the area with clear planning, communication, and practical logistics before and during treatment.

Travel and lodging checklist

Before booking

  • Complete intake forms and records transfer
  • Confirm candidacy and schedule window
  • Review estimated total visit duration

Lodging options

  • Hotels within 10-20 minutes of clinic
  • Extended-stay properties for 1+ week visits
  • Quiet rooms recommended between treatment days

Local transport

  • Rideshare and taxi availability
  • Parking details for private vehicles
  • Optional support person accompaniment

From inquiry to follow-up

  1. 1. Initial inquiry

    Call, email, or submit a web form with your basic clinical and travel information.

  2. 2. Record review and consultation

    Our team reviews psychiatric history, confirms fit, and discusses protocol options.

  3. 3. Travel coordination

    Receive your treatment calendar, suggested arrival time, and local stay guidance.

  4. 4. On-site treatment week

    Complete accelerated sessions with monitoring and scheduled clinician updates.

  5. 5. Transition home

    We share summaries and follow-up recommendations with you and home providers.
