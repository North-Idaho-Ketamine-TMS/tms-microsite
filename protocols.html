Protocol options

Standard accelerated protocol

Multiple daily sessions over 5 days, with structured breaks and daily physician review.

  • Typical candidates: treatment-resistant depression
  • Daily symptom and tolerability tracking
  • End-of-week progress conference

Modified accelerated protocol

Adjusted pacing for patients who benefit from fewer daily sessions or additional observation between sessions.

  • Flexible intensity options
  • Extended schedule over 7-10 days
  • Coordination with existing outpatient providers

Frequently asked questions

Is accelerated TMS safe?

TMS is generally well tolerated when appropriately screened and supervised. Your clinical team reviews medical history and risk factors before treatment begins.

How quickly can I start?

Start timing depends on intake completion, records review, and candidacy confirmation. Many patients can begin soon after consultation.

Can I continue current medications?

In many cases yes, but medication decisions are individualized and coordinated with your psychiatrist.

What happens after the intensive phase?

We provide a structured follow-up plan that may include maintenance care, therapy continuation, and communication with your home providers.
